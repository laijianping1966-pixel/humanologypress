About Humanology Press

Canadian Humanology Press Inc. is an independent Canadian publishing initiative dedicated to original inquiry into human nature, freedom, value, desire, will and civilization.

加拿大人学出版社致力于出版关于人性、自由、价值、需要、欲望、意志与文明问题的原创思想作品。

Books

《论需要与欲望——〈人学原理〉第一部》

On Needs and Desires: Principles of Humanology, Volume I

This work proposes “I want, therefore I am” as a fundamental proposition for understanding human existence, action, value and freedom.

本书以“我要故我在”为核心命题，重新探讨人的需要、欲望、价值观、意志与自由。

Author / Founder

Jianping Lai is the founder of Canadian Humanology Press Inc. and the author of On Needs and Desires.

赖建平，加拿大华裔独立学者，加拿大人学出版社创办人，《论需要与欲望》作者。
