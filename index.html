About Humanology Press

Canadian Humanology Press Inc. is an independent Canadian publishing initiative dedicated to original inquiry into human nature, freedom, value, desire, will and civilization.

加拿大人学出版社致力于出版关于人性、自由、价值、需要、欲望、意志与文明问题的原创思想作品。

Founder

Jianping Lai is the founder of Canadian Humanology Press Inc. and the author of On Needs and Desires: Principles of Humanology, Volume I.

赖建平，加拿大华裔独立学者，加拿大人学出版社创办人，《论需要与欲望》作者。

Vision

To advance original inquiry into human nature, value, freedom and civilization through rigorous scholarship, independent thinking and responsible publishing.

以独立思想与严肃出版推动关于人性、价值、自由与文明问题的原创研究。
